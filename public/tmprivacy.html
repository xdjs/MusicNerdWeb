Privacy Policy for Trail Mix

Last updated: October 02, 2025

Trail Mix respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • •
    No personal data. Trail Mix does not collect, store, or transmit your name, email, or any other personal information.
  • •
    No Bandcamp credentials. Your Bandcamp login credentials are never collected or stored by Trail Mix. Authentication is handled directly by Bandcamp.
  • •
    No browsing activity. Trail Mix does not monitor or log the websites you visit.
  • •
    No analytics. Trail Mix does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or third-party analytics.

How the Extension Works

  • • Trail Mix uses your existing Bandcamp account to download your purchased music.
  • • All operations happen locally in your browser.
  • • The extension only requests the minimal permissions required for downloads.

Data Sharing

Since Trail Mix does not collect or store data, no information is shared with third parties.

Open Source

Trail Mix is open source software. You can view the source code, report issues, or contribute improvements at: https://github.com/xdjs/TrailMix
