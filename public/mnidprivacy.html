Privacy Policy for Music Nerd ID

Effective Date: August 2025

Music Nerd ID respects your privacy. This policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. However, the app may access the following solely for functionality:

  • Microphone Access: Used only to recognize audio with ShazamKit. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers.
  • Internet Access: Required to query Shazam's catalog via Apple's services.

2. Data Handling

  • All song recognition is performed locally using Apple's ShazamKit.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

  • We rely on Apple ShazamKit for song identification.
  • We do not integrate advertising or analytics SDKs.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to modify or delete. If you have concerns, contact us.
